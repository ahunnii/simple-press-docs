## Overview

SimplePress is a free to use platform. We are a super small team and we are doing our best to provide a great experience for you. We are not a fortune 500 company and we are not going to be able to provide you with the same level of support as a larger company. We are a small team of developers and we are doing our best to provide a great experience for you.

## What you can expect

- We will be available to help you with any questions or issues you have. We will do our best to respond within 24 hours, but if we don't, please send a follow up email.
- We will continue to work with you to get your site up and running.
- We will be constantly updating the platform to improve the experience for you.

## What we can't promise

- We cannot promise that we will be able to add every feature you need. SimplePress is supposed to be a minimalistic platform. If you need more features, we can discuss other options with you.
- We cannot promise that issues, bugs, or features will be resolved in a timely manner. We have to triage issues based on our current work load with all of our clients and ongoing projects.
