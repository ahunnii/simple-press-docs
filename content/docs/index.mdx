**SimplePress** is a multi-tenant storefront platform: you get a hosted shop on a subdomain (and optionally a custom domain) plus a full **admin** dashboard to run products, orders, branding, and settings. These docs are written for **store owners and admins** using SimplePress day to day—not for end customers shopping your site.

<Callout type="info" title="Sign in and verification">

Sign in at `yourdomain.com/auth/sign-in`. **Email verification is required** before the admin at `/admin` is available. If you just signed up, check your inbox and complete verification first.

</Callout>

## Admin vs storefront

- **Admin** (`/admin`): where you configure products, orders, customers, discounts, content, and store settings.
- **Storefront**: the public site shoppers visit—the template, branding, and content you configure in admin.

## How to use these docs

Topics follow a practical order: onboarding and the **Dashboard**, then **Storefront** and commerce features, then **Settings**, and finally **Your account & security**. Use the sidebar to move sequentially or jump to any page. Use search when you know the task (for example “tax” or “domain”) but not which section it lives in.
